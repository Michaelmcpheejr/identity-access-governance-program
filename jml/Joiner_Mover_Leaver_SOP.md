# Joiner-Mover-Leaver (JML) SOP

## Joiner (Onboarding)

- Manager submits onboarding ticket
- IAM provisions access through RBAC groups
- Manager confirms access within 5 business days

Evidence:
- Ticket ID
- Group assignment record

---

## Mover (Role Change)

- Access change ticket required
- Old access removed, new access granted
- Privileged roles require separate approval

Evidence:
- Before/after membership snapshot

---

## Leaver (Offboarding)

- HR submits termination ticket immediately
- Account disabled within SLA
- Privileged roles stripped immediately

Evidence:
- Disablement timestamp
- Audit log entry
